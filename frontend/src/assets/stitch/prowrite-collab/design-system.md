## Brand

Focus & Flow is a corporate, modern, minimal design system for high-performance productivity tools. It favors precise alignment, generous whitespace, restrained color, and a distraction-free editor workspace.

## Component Guidance

- Primary buttons use `#2563eb` with white text.
- Secondary buttons use white surfaces, `#e5e7eb` borders, and dark gray text.
- Inputs use a 1px border and a blue focus glow.
- Toolbar icons are 20px, monochrome by default, and turn primary blue on hover or active state.
- Dashboard cards use white surfaces, 1px borders, and 24px internal padding.
- Collaborative sidebars use soft secondary backgrounds and circular avatars with presence dots.
- Presence cursors use a 2px vertical line and a compact name label.
